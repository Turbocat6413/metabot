No data *of extension users* whatsoever is collected, used or shared by the extension.

In order to highlight tweets of previously identified "Russian orchestrated human trolls for hire", account names and IDs *of those trolls* are *received* by the extension from a public web-hosted file on GitHub (https://github.com/antibot4navalny/accounts_labelled/blob/main/labels.json) and stored in browser's local storage.

No data of *other Twitter users* is scraped, collected or transmitted by the extension.

The extension does *not* handle user-related data in any other way.
